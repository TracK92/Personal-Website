About Me

Programming to me feels like Magic. I love the ability it gives me to create anything that I can imagine. I relish the challenges and frustrations of crafting something useful and personal through coding and the sheer elation that follows when I overcome them. Changing careers has not been easy. However, until I started coding, I had never been thoroughly excited about an occupation before. This is how I know that the world of software development is where I belong. Teaching myself how to code has introduced me to my latent curiosity and creativity. I have discovered that I am tenacious when it comes to problem-solving. I identify as a lifelong learner and working in a field that requires me to continually learn and solve problems is ideal. Personally, I have a passion for technology, books, food and people. I constantly strive for perfection in my work.
